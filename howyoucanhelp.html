How You Can Help Us

History

Author: Yaocheng "Saraline" Xia

A few of our members have a long history volunteering at Hana Mission. During Christmas, we pack gifts and deliver them to Hana Mission ourselves. These gifts includes stationaries and toys for children, shampoos and bodywashes for adults, and candies and snacks for all ages.



We bring the entire Thanksgiving feast to the needing table of Hana Mission every year, too!



Current Involvement

Setting up the thrift store

Author: Yaocheng "Saraline" Xia

Founders of clothes placed for revival frequently goes to Hana Mission thrift store at Paterson, NJ to help set up the store front.



From wipping down the windows, to sorting, pricing and hanging up the clothes, our hands are everywhere!



One of our tasks is renovation. Chloe J. and Charissa S. lead our renovation team. Under their direction, we re-decorate this space into a chic, trendy fashion store! Learn more about their renovation team here.
