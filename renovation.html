Hana Mission Clothing Booth

Author: Chloe Jacobson, Charissa Soh

The Hana Mission Clothing booth will be reselling used clothing in a thrift store fashion. There will be multiple clothing racks filled with a large selection of items in different sizes, styles, and designs. The profits of this fundraiser will go towards benefitting and growing our foundation. The money will be used to support the needs of the thrift store based in Paterson, NJ.

Thrift Shop Renovation Project

Author: Chloe Jacobson, Charissa Soh

The Thrift Shop renovation aims to create a welcoming and eye catching look to the store. Currently there is no color scheme, design, or name to the shop. However, these renovations will change that. The store will be painted with a Tiffany blue color that will be visible from any stop in town. There will also be a display of the clothing in the windows, along with a large store name above the entrance of the shop. These new features will attract more people and give the store a more comforting feel.
